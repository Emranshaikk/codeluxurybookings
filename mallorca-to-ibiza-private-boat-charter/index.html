6. FAQs: Mallorca to Ibiza Private Boat Charter

How long does the Mallorca to Ibiza private boat Charter crossing take?

A fast motor yacht takes 2.5-3 hours. A sailing catamaran spread the journey over 6-8 hours depending on wind conditions and anchor stops.

Can I do a Mallorca to Ibiza private boat Charter day trip?

Yes, a one-day round trip is entirely possible on a fast motor yacht or speedboat. Depart Mallorca at 8-9am and return in the early evening.

Do I need a sailing licence?

No, most charters are skippered by a professional captain. If you wish to bareboat charter, you will need a recognised qualification such as RYA Day Skipper.
