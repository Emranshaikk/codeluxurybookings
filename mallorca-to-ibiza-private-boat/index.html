Mallorca to Ibiza: The Luxury Private Boat Experience

Curated by the Elite Luxury Balearic Concierge Desk

While standard ferries connect the Balearic Islands, nothing compares to the **Mallorca to Ibiza private boat experience**. Crossing the 45-nautical-mile stretch of the Balearic Sea from your own private deck offers a level of exclusivity that transforms transportation into a journey.

Crossing Times & Vessel Choices

The duration of your crossing depends entirely on the vessel you select and the sea conditions. Generally, you can expect the following transit times from **Puerto Portals (Mallorca)** to **Marina Ibiza**:

  • Performance Motor Yacht (20+ knots): 3 - 4 hours. Absolute luxury for day crossings.
  • Luxury Catamaran (8-10 knots): 6 - 8 hours. Ideal for overnight voyages or slow-travel experiences.
  • Sailing Yacht: 7 - 10 hours. The pure, sustainable way to experience the Mediterranean.
