FAQ DESK

Private Island Rentals: Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a private island rental cost?

Rates for private island buyouts range from $20,000 to over $250,000 per night. Pricing is determined by the size of the island, manifest guest count, staffing structures, private yacht pairings, aviation clearances, and culinary demands. Peak season (winter and holiday enclaves) commands higher premium allocations.

Can I rent an island for an intimate honeymoon?

Yes. Intimate islands specifically configured for couples and honeymoons offer isolated luxury. We coordinate secluded overwater villas, couples spa regimes, sandbar candlelit dining, private marine charters, and remote flight arrivals for total romantic focus.

Are private islands available worldwide?

Yes. Elite Luxury Bookings operates an extensive off-market directory of premium islands across the Maldives, Fiji, Bahamas, Caribbean, Seychelles, Indonesia, and French Polynesia.

Can private islands accommodate large groups?

Yes. Destination wedding islands, family retreat estates, and corporate retreat islands are equipped to accommodate anywhere from 12 to 120 guests across multiple beachfront estates, overwater villas, and private lodges.

What luxury services are included in an all-inclusive private island rental?

Standard buyouts feature private Michelin-starred chefs, full spa and wellness staff, private dive masters, water sports teams, security personnel, senior lifestyle managers, custom dining arrangements, and ground crew handling.

How far in advance should I book?

To secure slot-clearances, flight manifests, yacht positionings, and seasonal peak windows, we highly recommend inquiring 6 to 12 months in advance, especially for summer and winter holidays.

Are private islands suitable for corporate retreats?

Yes. Private islands offer complete security, non-disclosure compliance, high-capacity boardrooms, executive beachfront villas, structured wellness tracks, and custom dining setups ideal for secure corporate alignment summits, board meetings, and product launches.
