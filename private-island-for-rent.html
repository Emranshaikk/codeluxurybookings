FAQ DESK

Private Island Rentals: Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a private island rental cost?

Rates for private island buyouts range from $20,000 to over $250,000 per night. Pricing depends on the size of the island, guest count, staffing requirements, yacht coordination, transfers, and culinary requests. Peak travel seasons (such as the holidays or summer periods) typically see higher booking rates.

Can I rent an island for an intimate honeymoon?

Yes. Intimate islands specifically curated for couples offer complete privacy. We coordinate luxury villa stays, couples spa treatments, sandbar candlelit dining, private boat excursions, and seamless transfers.

Are private islands available worldwide?

Yes. Elite Luxury Bookings connects clients with vetted partners offering premium island rentals across the Maldives, Fiji, Bahamas, Caribbean, Seychelles, Indonesia, and French Polynesia.

Can private islands accommodate large groups?

Yes. Many partner islands are equipped to accommodate larger groups, ranging from 12 to 120 guests, across multiple beachfront villas, suites, and private lodges.

What luxury services are included in an all-inclusive private island rental?

Most private island rentals include dedicated staff such as private chefs, villa managers, water sports instructors, spa therapists, and housekeeping to ensure a fully serviced, seamless experience.

How far in advance should I book?

To secure peak seasonal dates and coordinate custom travel logistics like yacht transfers and private aviation, we recommend booking 6 to 12 months in advance.

Are private islands suitable for corporate retreats?

Yes. Private islands are highly suited for executive retreats and board meetings. They offer complete privacy, professional meeting facilities, customized dining arrangements, and a distraction-free environment.
