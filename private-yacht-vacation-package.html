Sovereign Luxury: What Is a Private Yacht Vacation Package?

Step away from the static confines of land-based resorts. A private yacht vacation package is a fully tailored, mobile sanctuary that places you, your family, or your executive team at the center of a personalized maritime travel experience.

Unlike traditional five-star hotels where you share facilities, beaches, and dining halls with hundreds of other guests, a private yacht holiday package grants you exclusive ownership of your space, staff, and itinerary. Every aspect of a yacht getaway is customized. From the moment you step onto the teak deck, you are surrounded by a world designed specifically for you. The yacht operates as your private floating villa, complete with spacious staterooms, dining salons, deck lounges, and beach clubs, moving seamlessly between remote islands and historic harbors at your command.

A true yacht vacation package is fully serviced and managed by a dedicated, professional crew. Safe passage, culinary craft, and guest comfort are handled by specialists. Our crewed charters feature master captains with extensive local knowledge, gourmet private chefs, attentive stewardesses, and deckhands ready to deploy water sports equipment. By selecting a crewed yacht vacation, you receive five-star service tailored to your party's schedule. This makes it an ideal vacation choice for luxury travelers looking to relax in complete comfort.

When you book a private yacht vacation package, you choose convenience and peace of mind. Our concierges handle all logistics prior to boarding. We pre-plan custom itineraries, source specific wines and gourmet foods, clear maritime port slots, and coordinate VIP airport transfers. This turnkey approach provides a worry-free experience, allowing you to relax while our crew delivers exceptional service. This represents the ultimate way to enjoy the ocean, far exceeding the boundaries of traditional luxury hotels.

The Superyacht Advantage: Why Choose a Private Yacht Vacation?

For those who value time, autonomy, and security, a private yacht charter vacation represents the ultimate form of travel. Discover the unique benefits of choosing a private vessel over traditional land-based luxury travel.

Complete Privacy & Security

Onboard your yacht, your space is entirely your own. Surrounded by miles of open ocean, you are shielded from the public eye. Our crews are trained under strict non-disclosure policies, offering a secure environment for families, high-profile couples, and corporate boards.

Personalized, Flexible Itineraries

You are not bound by fixed flight schedules or tour itineraries. If you discover a quiet cove in the Caribbean or a charming village in the French Riviera, you can instruct the captain to drop anchor. Your schedule remains fully flexible, adapting to your desires and the weather.

Access to Exclusive Destinations

Many of the world's most beautiful bays, shallow coral reefs, and exclusive harbors are inaccessible to cruise ships and land travelers. Your private yacht can navigate shallow channels, anchor in quiet lagoons, and dock in boutique ports, placing you in the heart of pristine coastlines.

Luxury Floating Accommodations

Modern superyachts are built with high-end craftsmanship. Enjoy master cabins with panoramic ocean views, en-suite marble bathrooms, multi-deck salons, private jacuzzi tubs, outdoor dining decks, and state-of-the-art stabilizers that ensure a smooth ride while at anchor or underway.

Bespoke Gourmet Dining

Your dining experiences are designed around your taste, dietary restrictions, and allergies. Your private chef coordinates menus prior to departure, utilizing fresh local fish, organic vegetables, and premium ingredients to create Michelin-level meals served on deck at sunset.

Dedicated VIP Service

With guest-to-crew ratios often approaching 1:1, you receive highly personalized service. The crew quickly learns your preferences—whether it's how you take your morning coffee, your favorite watersports activities, or your preferred cocktail, ensuring a tailored experience.

Elite Cruising Grounds: Best Destinations for Private Yacht Vacations

Explore the world's most beautiful destinations aboard a private superyacht. Our global charter portfolio features year-round cruising options in pristine waters, each offering unique local appeal, rich itineraries, and fine dining.

The Caribbean

A tropical paradise of white-sand beaches, warm trade winds, and vibrant marine life. Ideal for winter and spring yacht vacations, offering excellent snorkeling, beach picnics, and easy island hopping.

  • Highlights: British Virgin Islands, St. Barts, Anguilla.
  • Best Season: December to April.
  • Link: Caribbean Charters

The Bahamas

Shallow, turquoise waters and sandbars located a short distance from Florida. Perfect for family vacations, diving with nurse sharks, exploring coral reefs, and relaxing in secluded anchorages.

  • Highlights: Exuma Cays, Harbour Island, Abacos.
  • Best Season: November to May.
  • Link: Bahamas Guide

The Mediterranean

The global center of luxury summer yachting, combining historic port towns, fine dining, and beautiful coastlines. Perfect for travelers seeking culture, beach clubs, and scenic coastal cruising.

  • Highlights: Amalfi Coast, Sardinia, Corsica.
  • Best Season: June to September.
  • Link: Mediterranean Guide

French Riviera

Experience the glamour, high-end shopping, and beach clubs of Saint-Tropez, Cannes, and Monaco. Ideal for guests looking to combine luxury yachting with fine dining and events.

  • Highlights: Monaco Harbor, St. Tropez, Cannes.
  • Best Season: May to September.
  • Link: Monaco Travel

The Greek Islands

Explore whitewashed villages, ancient history, and blue waters. Perfect for custom island hopping, enjoying fresh seafood in local tavernas, and watching stunning sunsets.

  • Highlights: Mykonos, Santorini, Ionian Sea.
  • Best Season: May to October.
  • Link: Greece Masterclass

Croatia & Montenegro

Navigate medieval fortress towns, quiet pine-forested islands, and clean waters. Perfect for history lovers, nature hikes, and quiet anchorages in secluded bays.

  • Highlights: Dubrovnik, Hvar, Kotor Bay.
  • Best Season: June to September.
  • Link: Montenegro Guide

The Maldives

Prinstine coral atolls, private sandbanks, and overwater villas. Excellent for luxury scuba diving, surfing, spa retreats, and absolute privacy in the Indian Ocean.

  • Highlights: Baa Atoll, Ari Atoll, private sandbar dining.
  • Best Season: November to April.
  • Link: Maldives Escape

Dubai & Arabian Gulf

Modern superyachting set against an impressive skyline. Ideal for urban cruises, high-end corporate retreats, private parties, and VIP group gatherings.

  • Highlights: Dubai Marina, Palm Jumeirah, World Islands.
  • Best Season: October to April.
  • Link: Dubai Yachting

Private yacht vacation package pricing varies based on the size, build year, and category of the yacht, as well as the destination and season. Base weekly charter costs generally range from $50,000 for a luxury catamaran to over $500,000 for massive motor megayachts. In a standard package, additional expenses are managed via an Advanced Provisioning Allowance (APA), typically 30-35% of the base charter fee, covering fuel, food, beverages, and dockage fees.
