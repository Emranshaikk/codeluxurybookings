Why Choose a Catamaran for Your 2026 Charter?

The "double-hull" design provides a level of comfort that makes it ideal for families and those new to sailing. With the salon and cabins often spread across two hulls, privacy and social space are perfectly balanced.

FAQs: Catamaran Rental Guide

Do I need a license to rent a catamaran?

For a "bareboat" charter, yes. Most European countries require an ICC (International Certificate of Competence) or equivalent. However, most elite clients opt for a Skippered Charter to ensure local expertise and maximum relaxation.

How many people can fit on a standard 45ft catamaran?

Most modern 45ft designs feature 4 double cabins with ensuite bathrooms, comfortably accommodating 8 guests plus 2 crew members in peak comfort.

Is fuel included in the price?

Generally, no. For catamarans, fuel is usually part of the Advance Provisioning Allowance (APA), which covers operating costs during your journey.
