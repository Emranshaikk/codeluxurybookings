Your Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a private island honeymoon rental cost?
Pricing for an exclusive private island honeymoon rental typically ranges from $15,000 to over $150,000 per night, depending on the coordinates, buyout exclusivity, staffing, and logistical requirements (like seaplane or yacht tenders). Whole-island buyouts for ultimate romantic privacy generally require a minimum booking of 5 to 7 nights, bringing the weekly investment range to $100,000 to $1,000,000+.
What is included in a honeymoon island rental?
A fully-serviced private island rental typically includes absolute exclusive use of the entire island, a private beachfront pool villa or overwater estate, 24/7 dedicated butler service, customized dining by Michelin-trained private chefs, premium bars, standard non-motorized water sports (snorkeling, paddleboarding, kayaking), housekeeping, and roundtrip yacht or helicopter airport transfers.
Which destinations are best for a private island honeymoon?
The best destinations vary by your travel preferences. For iconic overwater luxury and vibrant reefs, the Maldives is unrivaled. For rapid travel times from the Americas and sapphire-blue waters, the Bahamas Exuma Cays are ideal. For ancient granite cliffs and lush jungles, the Seychelles represents the peak of eco-luxury. Fiji and French Polynesia are perfect for volcano landscapes and rich sailing heritages.
Are private island honeymoon packages all-inclusive?
Yes. When booking through Elite Luxury Bookings, our premium honeymoon plans are structured as fully integrated cays. All gourmet custom meals, premium alcohol, and house activities are covered. Specific ultra-premium excursions (like deep-water yacht charters, helicopter rides, or high-end vintage wine list access) can be pre-funded under an Advance Provisioning Allowance (APA).
Can private jets be arranged?
Absolutely. Through our integrated aviation department, we coordinate door-to-door romantic transit. Depart from any executive private airport terminal in the world, fly on a customized jet charter, and arrive securely via helicopter or yacht directly to the private island.
How far in advance should couples book?
To secure the most exclusive off-market island cays and optimal weather windows (especially during dry winter seasons or high-demand holiday slots), we strongly advise inquiring **6 to 12 months in advance**. Last-minute procurement is sometimes possible for verified clients, subject to coordinate clearance.
Are honeymoon island rentals available year-round?
Yes. Because we source private islands globally, there is always a spectacular tropical corridor in peak season. When the Caribbean and Bahamas experience rainfall or humidity in autumn, the South Pacific and Indian Ocean are in pristine dry season, and vice versa. Your concierge will guide you to the perfect location for your specific wedding dates.
