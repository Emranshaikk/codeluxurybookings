FAQ DESK

Caribbean Private Island Rentals: Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Caribbean private island rental cost?

Rates for private island buyouts range from $20,000 to over $250,000 per night. Pricing is determined by the size of the island, manifest guest count, staffing structures, private yacht pairings, aviation clearances, and culinary demands. Peak season (winter and holiday enclaves) commands higher premium allocations.

Can I rent an entire private island in the Caribbean?

Yes. All reservations made through Elite Luxury Bookings are for exclusive-use private island rentals. The entire island, all its accommodations, staff, and fleets are dedicated solely to you and your manifest guests.

Which Caribbean islands offer the most luxury?

The most luxurious enclaves include the Exuma Cays in the Bahamas (for proximity to the US and mega-yacht docking), the British Virgin Islands (Necker & Moskito), Turks & Caicos (beachfront estates), Saint Lucia (volcanic peaks), and the Grenadines (secluded sailing retreats).

Are private island rentals all-inclusive?

Yes, buyouts are fully configured as all-inclusive experiences. Everything from your private Michelin-starred chefs, water sports instruction, custom yacht excursions, and wellness treatments is included in a single, secure buyout rate.

Can private jets be arranged?

Yes. Elite Luxury Bookings operates an integrated mobility desk that coordinates private jet charters, custom flight decks, and direct helicopter transfers landing directly within the secure perimeter of your private island helipad.

How far in advance should I book?

To secure slot-clearances, flight manifests, yacht positionings, and seasonal peak windows, we highly recommend inquiring 6 to 12 months in advance, especially for the summer and winter holidays.

Are Caribbean private islands suitable for weddings and retreats?

Absolutely. Private islands offer complete security, non-disclosure compliance, high-capacity beachfront dining halls, guest villas, close security corridors, and complete marine operations ideal for destination weddings, family reunions, and corporate retreat summits.
