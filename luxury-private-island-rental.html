FAQ DESK

Luxury Private Island Rentals: Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a luxury private island rental cost?

Rates for private island buyouts range from $25,000 to over $300,000 per night. Pricing is determined by the size of the island, manifest guest count, staffing structures, private yacht pairings, aviation clearances, and culinary demands. Peak season (winter and holiday enclaves) commands higher premium allocations.

What is included in a luxury island rental?

Standard buyouts feature private Michelin-starred chefs, full spa and wellness staff, private dive masters, water sports teams, security personnel, senior lifestyle managers, custom dining arrangements, and ground crew handling.

Can I rent an island exclusively?

Yes. All reservations made through Elite Luxury Bookings are for exclusive-use private island rentals. The entire island, all its accommodations, staff, and fleets are dedicated solely to you and your manifest guests.

Are luxury island rentals all-inclusive?

Yes, buyouts are fully configured as all-inclusive experiences. Everything from your personalized menus, wellness sessions, water sports instruction, and local marine charters is included in a single, secure buyout rate.

Which destinations offer the most luxurious private islands?

We operate an active directory of premium islands across the Maldives (overwater villas), the Bahamas (proximity to the US with deep-water cays), the Caribbean (Necker & private cays), the Seychelles (granite beach isolation), and Fiji (eco-luxury sanctuaries).

Can private jets be arranged?

Yes. Elite Luxury Bookings operates an integrated mobility desk that coordinates private jet charters, custom flight decks, and direct helicopter transfers landing directly within the secure perimeter of your private island helipad.
