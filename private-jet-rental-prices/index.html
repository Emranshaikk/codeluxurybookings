Hidden Costs: The "Bill-Back" Factors

When you receive a quote, the hourly rate is just the start. You must always account for these additional factors:

  • Repositioning Fees (Ferry Flights): The cost of moving the empty jet to your pickup destination.
  • Landing Fees & FBO Handling: Major airports like **Teterboro** or **Paris Le Bourget** carry premium handling charges.
  • Hangar & De-icing: Winter missions can carry unplanned costs exceeding $5,000 if de-icing is required before take-off.
  • Wait Time & Overnight Fees: If your mission requires the aircraft to stay on the ground for several days.

ROI: Evaluating Value vs. Price

Elite clients focus on **ROI, not just the ticket price**. The real value is found in the **elimination of ground transit time** and the preservation of **executive energy**. By using FBOs, you can arrive 15 minutes before departure, bypassing the hours lost in security and public terminals.

FAQs: Jet Rental Pricing

Does the price change during peak events?

Yes. During events like the **Monaco Grand Prix** or the **Cannes Film Festival**, aircraft availability drops significantly, and market rates can increase by 20-40%.

What happens if I cancel?

Cancellation policies vary by operator. Generally, a **non-refundable deposit** is required. Within 48-72 hours of departure, penalties often scale to 100% of the mission cost.

Is catering included?

Standard cabin refreshments and snacks are included. Bespoke gourmet catering from Michelin-starred partners is a "bill-back" item based on your specific requests.
