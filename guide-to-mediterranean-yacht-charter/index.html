Understanding Costs & The APA Model

Pricing for a luxury Mediterranean yacht charter operates on the Advance Provisioning Allowance (APA) model. The base charter fee covers the vessel and crew, while the APA (typically 30-40% of the base fee) covers fuel, food, wine, and port fees. At the flagship tier, a 50-metre motor yacht typically ranges from $150,000 to $300,000 per week before expenses.

FAQs: Mediterranean Yacht Charter

How much does it cost to charter a private yacht?

Base fees range from $40,000 for luxury sailing vessels to $2,000,000+ per week for flagship superyachts. Itemized quotes will include APA and local VAT.

What is the best country to visit?

Greece and Italy are consistently rated as the premier destinations due to their incredible diversity of experience within a single sailing area.

How far in advance should I plan?

For prime positioning during events like the Monaco Grand Prix or Cannes Film Festival, plan 12-18 months in advance to secure the highest-tier vessels.
