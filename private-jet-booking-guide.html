Save Up to 75% With Empty Legs

Empty leg flights are one of aviation's best-kept secrets. Here's everything you need to know.

What is an Empty Leg?

When a private jet needs to reposition — flying from its last drop-off to its next pickup with no passengers — operators offer these seats at steep discounts of 25–75%. The aircraft flies regardless; you benefit from the savings.

Is it the Same Experience?

Absolutely. The same aircraft, crew, catering, and FBO experience. The only difference is the price and that routes are fixed. Perfect for flexible travellers whose schedule aligns with the repositioning flight.

How Do I Book One?

Submit your route and date in our form. Our team cross-references your request against repositioning flights in our network. If an empty leg matches, we'll include it prominently in your 3 quotes.

Who Is It Best For?

Leisure travellers with flexible dates, corporate teams doing one-way trips, and anyone who has used commercial first class and wants the private terminal experience without the full charter price.

Private Jet Charter Cost Guide

Understanding private jet charter cost in 2026 requires looking beyond the hourly rate. While a Light Jet might start at $3,500/hour, the true all-in cost includes aircraft repositioning (also called "deadhead" costs), landing fees, ground handling, crew accommodation on multi-day trips, and fuel surcharges that fluctuate with jet-A prices. Our platform eliminates these surprises by building every variable into your quote before you commit.

What Drives Private Jet Charter Prices?

The biggest cost drivers are route distance (which determines fuel burn), aircraft category (heavier jets cost more per hour but may be more cost-efficient per passenger on large groups), and positioning — whether the aircraft is already at your departure airport or needs to reposition from elsewhere. Booking a jet that is already positioned at your origin airport is the most reliable way to reduce your overall charter cost by 10–25% compared to a random broker quote.

Seasonal demand also affects pricing. Q4 (October–December) tends to see elevated charter rates due to holiday traffic and ski season demand into the Alps and Rockies. February–March and late August often present better availability and more competitive rates on popular leisure routes.

How to Book a Private Jet at the Best Rate

The most effective way to book a private jet at a discount is via empty leg flights, which can save 25–75% on standard rates. For flexibility, booking 48–72 hours in advance (rather than same-day) also opens more positioning options and typically yields better pricing. For last-minute urgent departures, our WhatsApp concierge line ensures aircraft availability is confirmed within 15 minutes.

Comparing Aircraft Categories for Your Mission

For short regional trips — London to Paris, Dubai to Doha, or New York to Boston — a Light Jet such as the Cessna Citation CJ4 or Embraer Phenom 300 delivers maximum efficiency. Seat 6 passengers comfortably, fast turnaround, access to smaller airstrips. For transatlantic routes or when a working boardroom environment is essential, a Heavy Jet such as the Gulfstream G650 or Bombardier Global 7500 provides intercontinental range, flat-bed sleeping, full galley service, and the privacy required by high-level principals.

Understanding FBO Experience & Ground Handling

One of the most overlooked advantages of private aviation is the Fixed Base Operator (FBO) experience. You arrive at a dedicated private terminal — no check-in queues, no public security lines, no gate waiting. Depending on the FBO, you drive directly to the aircraft steps. All Elite Luxury Bookings quotes include handling at premium FBOs such as Signature Aviation, Jet Aviation, and TAG at major hubs worldwide.
