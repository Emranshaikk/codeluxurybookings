Maritime Intelligence

Frequently asked questions for the discerning traveler.

How much does a luxury yacht rental cost?

Daily charters begin from $10,000, while mega yacht week rentals average $250,000+. Costs are influenced by vessel size, crew count, and seasonal positioning.

Can I host a helicopter on the yacht?

Yes, our Helideck-equipped Mega Yacht fleet supports helicopter landing and storage for seamless ship-to-shore transitions.

What is included in the charter fee?

Fees include the vessel, a professional crew, and standard luxury amenities. APA (Advance Provisioning Allowance) covers fuel, catering, and specific guest requests.
