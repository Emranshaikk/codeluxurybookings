Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a yacht rental cost?

The cost of a luxury yacht rental varies greatly depending on the size of the vessel, the destination, and duration. Daily charters can range from $3,000 for standard yachts to over $50,000 for mega yachts. Weekly VIP charters average between $40,000 to over $1,000,000 for the world's most luxurious superyachts.

Can I host a wedding on a yacht?

Absolutely. Yacht weddings are the pinnacle of exclusivity. Many mega yachts and specialized sailing catamarans are equipped to host 50 to 150+ guests, offering fully catered dining, ballroom spaces, and breathtaking ocean backdrops.

What is included in a yacht charter?

A standard luxury yacht charter includes the vessel, an experienced captain, and a dedicated crew. Most premium charters also include water toys, snorkeling gear, and bespoke itinerary planning. Fuel and catering are calculated via an Advance Provisioning Allowance (APA).

Which destinations are best?

For summer events, the Mediterranean coastlines of Ibiza, Mallorca, Monaco, and the Amalfi Coast remain unmatched. For winter escapes and opulent corporate parties, Dubai and the Caribbean Islands offer spectacular weather and elite offshore infrastructures.
