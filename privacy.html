Privacy Protocol

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Commitment to Discretion

At Elite Luxury Bookings, we understand that privacy is the ultimate luxury. This protocol outlines how we manage and protect the sensitive mission intelligence you provide during the procurement of aviation, maritime, and estate services.

2. Data Collection & Intelligence

We only collect data essential for the safe and secure coordination of your travel requirements. This includes:

  • Personal identification for FBO and maritime manifests.
  • Secure communication details (WhatsApp, Signal, or encrypted email).
  • Specific mission requirements (itineraries, security preferences, dietary needs).

3. Security Standards

All client data is encrypted and stored in secure environments. We employ industry-standard protocols to prevent unauthorized access. For high-profile individuals, we coordinate with private security teams to ensure data handling meets your specific NDA requirements.

4. Third-Party Coordination

To execute your journey, we share essential data with vetted partners (aircraft operators, yacht owners, estate managers). These partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and only receive the intelligence necessary to fulfill their specific role in your mission.

5. Your Rights

You maintain full control over your data. You may request the immediate deletion of your mission records upon completion of your travel, subject to legal and regulatory aviation requirements.

6. Contact the Protocol Officer

For questions regarding your privacy or to request data removal, please contact our desk at: contact@eliteluxurybookings.com
