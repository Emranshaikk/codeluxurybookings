FAQs: Montenegro Yacht Charter

Is Montenegro part of the EU for VAT purposes?

Montenegro is not currently in the EU, which often allows for 0% VAT on yacht charters and duty-free fuel, making it a highly attractive destination for high-end charters compared to neighboring Croatia.

What is the "Black Mountain" experience?

It refers to the dramatic limestone mountains that rise directly from the sea. The name "Montenegro" literally translates to Black Mountain, and the best way to witness this scale is from the flybridge of your yacht in the middle of the bay.

How far is it to Dubrovnik, Croatia?

The border is approximately 30 nautical miles from Kotor. Many charters start in Montenegro to take advantage of the better fuel prices and then cruise into Croatian waters (subject to permit).
