Section 01

Nice Côte d'Azur (NCE):
The Gateway to Monaco

When you book a private jet to Monaco, your aircraft lands at Nice Côte d'Azur Airport (IATA: NCE) — the second busiest airport in France and the undisputed hub for private aviation on the French Riviera. Monaco has no commercial or private runway of its own; Nice is the professional answer to the question of how to arrive.

All private jet charter Monaco missions use the dedicated Terminal d'Aviation d'Affaires (Terminal 3) — completely separate from the commercial terminals. It features private customs and immigration clearance, personal lounges, and a ground team that coordinates directly with your helicopter transfer.

Terminal Intelligence

The Nice Business Aviation Terminal sits on a runway surrounded by water on three sides — arguably the most dramatic landing approach in European private aviation. Your aircraft parks steps from the helicopter pad.

For those seeking maximum discretion, Cannes-Mandelieu Airport (CEQ) offers an alternative point of entry — approximately 35 km west of Monaco. Note that CEQ has runway length and weight restrictions best suited to light jet charter to Monaco.

Section 02

The 7-Minute Bridge:
Nice to Monaco Helicopter Transfer

The defining feature of any private jet Monaco arrival is the helicopter connection. Rather than a 45-minute road transfer through Riviera traffic, your helicopter departs from a pad yards from your jet's stairs. In seven minutes, you cross from Nice to the Monaco Heliport in Fontvieille.

This is not a shuttle. This is the continuation of the journey. For clients chartering a private jet to Monaco for the Grand Prix, the aerial approach is as much a part of the experience as the event itself.

The Elite Protocol

At Elite Luxury Bookings, helicopter departure is synchronised with your jet's wheels-down time. There is no waiting on the tarmac. The transition from a 40,000ft intercontinental cruise to a Monte Carlo penthouse takes under fifteen minutes.

Night Arrival Note

The Monaco Heliport observes strict operating hours. For late-night arrivals, Elite Luxury Bookings arranges a dedicated luxury chauffeur transfer from Nice, typically 30–40 minutes.

Section 03

Grand Prix & Yacht Show:
Slot Priority Intelligence

During the Monaco Grand Prix private jet season and the Monaco Yacht Show, Nice Côte d'Azur Airport becomes one of the most contested airspaces in the world. Landing slots are finite. Demand from private jet hire Monaco clients across Europe, the Middle East, and the United States creates a bottleneck.

Elite Luxury Bookings operates a Slot Priority programme — our Riviera aviation desk secures preferred landing windows months in advance. For clients who intend to charter a private jet to Monaco Grand Prix 2026, we recommend engaging our team no later than January of that year.
