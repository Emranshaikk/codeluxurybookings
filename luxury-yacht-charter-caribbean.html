The Ultimate Sanctuary: Why Choose a Caribbean Yacht Charter?

Bypassing typical resorts and crowded beaches. A Caribbean yacht vacation offers complete freedom, luxury travel, and direct entry into areas that are unreachable by land.

When dreaming of tropical escapes, travelers look for clarity, beauty, and peace. A private yacht charter Caribbean provides exactly that, combined with the safety of a secure vessel. Discover the warm year-round tropical climate that makes these islands a top destination for winter travel. Enjoy warm sunshine and trade winds that create perfect cruising conditions from November through May.

Furthermore, the geography of the Caribbean features crystal-clear waters and white-sand beaches. Choosing a crewed yacht charter Caribbean gives you a mobile base to explore shallow sandbars, coral gardens, and quiet harbors. Your captain coordinates your itinerary, bypassing crowded spots in favor of private anchorages, empty beaches, and exclusive beach clubs.

Beyond natural beauty, the Caribbean yachting experience provides unmatched service. Onboard, a team of professional crew members—including hostesses, deckhands, and a private chef—attends to every detail. This personalized care ensures a comfortable, relaxed travel experience, allowing you and your family to unwind in complete privacy.

Prestigious Island Destinations: Top Caribbean Ports

Choose the perfect destinations for your Caribbean yacht getaway. From lively harbors to isolated coral islands, our itineraries explore the highlights of the Caribbean.

St. Barts

The pinnacle of Caribbean glamour and high-end lifestyle. St. Barts blends French luxury with tropical beauty, attracting celebrities, CEOs, and luxury travelers globally.

  • Luxury Appeal: Designer boutiques, exclusive beach clubs, vibrant nightlife, and luxury yacht lifestyle.
  • Popular Anchorages: Gustavia Harbor (for stern-to docking) and Colombier Bay (for quiet anchorage).
  • Beach Clubs & Dining: Nikki Beach St Barth, Gyp Sea, and Shellona for lunch; Bonito and L'Isola for dinner.
  • Ideal Traveler: VIP couples, luxury vacation seekers, and parties looking for chic entertainment.

British Virgin Islands

The global capital of sailing and protected cruising. The BVI features dozens of islands and islets located close together, offering easy island hopping yacht charter options.

  • Luxury Appeal: Calm water cruising, beautiful dive sites, and iconic beach bars.
  • Popular Anchorages: The Baths (Virgin Gorda), Cane Garden Bay (Tortola), and Great Harbour (Jost Van Dyke).
  • Beach Clubs & Dining: CocoMaya (Virgin Gorda), Soggy Dollar Bar (Jost Van Dyke), and Saba Rock.
  • Ideal Traveler: Families, sailing enthusiasts, and travelers looking for scenic cruising.

Antigua

Rich in sailing heritage and historic charm. Antigua features 365 beaches, historic naval dockyards, and sheltered anchorages, perfect for sailing vacations.

  • Luxury Appeal: Historic Nelson's Dockyard, world-class regattas, and secluded luxury resorts.
  • Popular Anchorages: Falmouth Harbour, English Harbour, and Green Island for snorkeling.
  • Beach Clubs & Dining: Catherine's Cafe, Sheer Rocks, and Rokuni for cliffside dining.
  • Ideal Traveler: Yachting enthusiasts, history lovers, and couples seeking romantic privacy.

Bahamas

The Exuma Cays feature 365 islands surrounded by shallow turquoise sandbars. The Bahamas offers private island beach setups, marine reserves, and shallow water cruising.

  • Luxury Appeal: Private islands, shallow turquoise waters, sandbar setups, and diving.
  • Popular Anchorages: Compass Cay (swim with nurse sharks), Warderick Wells, and Staniel Cay.
  • Beach Clubs & Dining: Staniel Cay Yacht Club, and private beach picnics prepared by your chef.
  • Ideal Traveler: Families with kids, watersports lovers, and VIP groups.

Turks & Caicos

Famed for Providenciales' Grace Bay and deep marine drop-offs. Turks & Caicos offers quiet coral islands, bonefishing, and premium dive walls.

  • Luxury Appeal: Award-winning beaches, luxury spas, bonefishing, and clear reef waters.
  • Popular Anchorages: Grace Bay, Pine Cay, and West Caicos for remote diving.
  • Beach Clubs & Dining: Da Conch Shack, Infiniti Restaurant & Raw Bar, and Grace's Cottage.
  • Ideal Traveler: Scuba divers, anglers, wellness seekers, and honeymoon couples.

Grenadines

A chain of small islands stretching south from St. Vincent. The Grenadines offers raw natural beauty, private islands, and marine reserves.

  • Luxury Appeal: Exclusive private islands (Mustique, Petit St. Vincent) and coral lagoons.
  • Popular Anchorages: Tobago Cays (swim with sea turtles), Salt Whistle Bay, and Mustique.
  • Beach Clubs & Dining: Basil's Bar (Mustique), Sandy Lane Yacht Club (Canouan), and beach setups.
  • Ideal Traveler: Celebrities seeking privacy, explorers, and nature lovers.

Anguilla

A flat coral island known for quiet luxury, dining, and white-sand bays. Anguilla is the perfect destination for travelers looking for relaxation.

  • Luxury Appeal: Award-winning dining, soft white sand, and high-end resorts.
  • Popular Anchorages: Road Bay, Sandy Island (a tiny offshore sandbar), and Prickly Pear Cays.
  • Beach Clubs & Dining: Blanchard's, Straw Hat, and Malliouhana's Celeste.
  • Ideal Traveler: Gourmands, relaxation seekers, and honeymoon couples.

U.S. Virgin Islands

St. Thomas and St. John feature deep harbors, direct access to US flight hubs, and protected National Park bays, perfect for family cruises.

  • Luxury Appeal: Duty-free shopping, direct flight access, and protected bays.
  • Popular Anchorages: Trunk Bay (St. John), Caneel Bay, and Christmas Cove.
  • Beach Clubs & Dining: The Lime Out (floating taco bar in St. John), ZoZo's at Caneel Bay.
  • Ideal Traveler: Families seeking easy travel, scuba divers, and shopping lovers.

The Fleet: Types of Yachts Available in the Caribbean

Select the perfect yacht type for your vacation. From large motor superyachts to stable luxury catamarans, we match you with the ideal vessel.

Superyachts

Vessels over 100 feet featuring premium amenities such as jacuzzis, heli-decks, stabilizers, movie theaters, and a large selection of water toys. Ideal for VIP events and large families.

Motor Yachts

Fast, spacious, and comfortable. Motor yachts cover large distances quickly, making them perfect for island hopping itineraries (e.g. cruising the Leeward or Windward Islands).

Sailing Yachts

Classic, romantic, and peaceful. Experience the excitement of sailing powered by the trade winds, combined with modern luxury cabins and crew service.

Catamarans

Stable, spacious, and shallow-draft. Catamarans feature wide decks, trampolines, and shallow drafts that allow them to anchor close to beaches and sandbars (e.g. in the Bahamas Cays).

Expedition Yachts

Built for long range travel and stability. Features steel hulls, long fuel ranges, and space for tenders, landing craft, and deep dive setups. Perfect for adventure trips.

Family-Friendly Yachts

Outfitted with child-safety netting, kid-friendly water toys (inflatable slides, towables), spacious twin cabins, and crews experienced in hosting families.

Custom Journeys: Sample Caribbean Itineraries

Get inspired by our curated itineraries. Every trip is customized to your preferences, seasonal weather patterns, and pace.

7-Day Luxury BVI Voyage

01

Embarkation in Tortola

Board your yacht at Nanny Cay Marina. Meet your captain and crew, enjoy welcome cocktails, and cruise to Christmas Cove for a quiet dinner anchorage.

02

The Baths to Virgin Gorda

Cruise to Virgin Gorda. Tender to The Baths to explore giant granite boulders and tidal pools. Enjoy lunch on deck before anchoring in Gorda Sound.

03

Anegada Island Hopping

Sail north to Anegada, a flat coral island. Explore white beaches, snorkel horseshoe reef, and enjoy local lobster at Loblolly Bay.

04

Sandy Spit to Jost Van Dyke

Anchor at Sandy Spit, a tiny island with palm trees. Spend the afternoon snorkeling before cruising to Great Harbour, Jost Van Dyke for dinner.

05

White Bay Beach Setup

Spend the day at White Bay, swimming to the Soggy Dollar Bar. Your crew sets up a private beach canopy, dining area, and water toys on the sand.

06

Norman Island Caves

Cruise to Norman Island. Snorkel the Caves (famed in Treasure Island) and the Indians reef before enjoying a sunset cocktail reception on deck.

07

Peter Island & Disembarkation

Enjoy a morning swim at Peter Island. Cruise back to Tortola for disembarkation and transfer to the airport.
