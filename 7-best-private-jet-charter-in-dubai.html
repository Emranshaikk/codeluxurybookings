1. Why Dubai is the Capital of Private Aviation

Dubai is not just a destination; it is a gateway. Its geographic position allows a Gulfstream G650ER to reach nearly any major city on earth with a single stop or, in many cases, direct. The UAE government's investment in dedicated aviation infrastructure has created a seamless environment where a passenger can transition from their limousine to their jet in under 5 minutes.

2. The VIP Terminals: DWC vs. DXB

Choosing the right airport is critical for your mission. While Dubai International (DXB) is more central, Al Maktoum International (DWC) is the future. DWC is home to the world's first purpose-built aerotropolis, offering multiple FBO (Fixed Base Operator) options that cater specifically to VIP and Royal missions.
