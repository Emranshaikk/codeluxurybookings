Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa to use the DWC VIP terminal in Dubai?

Accessing the VIP terminal at Al Maktoum International (DWC) does not bypass standard UAE immigration protocols. You must hold a valid visa or be eligible for a visa-on-arrival based on your nationality. The terminal provides a private, expedited passport control desk that completes the clearance process in minutes.

How far in advance should I book a private jet from Dubai?

While we can orchestrate flights in as little as two to four hours for urgent requirements, booking at least one to three days in advance is recommended. This allows our team to secure your preferred aircraft type, coordinate custom catering from local establishments, and guarantee optimal airport slots.

Can I bring pets on a private charter from Dubai?

Yes, pets can travel directly in the cabin with you on designated pet-friendly charters. You will need to provide valid vaccination records, pet passports, and UAE import/export permits. Our aviation desk handles the coordination with local customs authorities to ensure a seamless arrival.

What is the cheapest private jet option from Dubai to Riyadh?

The most cost-effective and efficient option for the Dubai-to-Riyadh route is a Light Jet, such as the Phenom 300E. This category provides an entry-level charter option for up to six passengers with a flight time of approximately ninety minutes. For enhanced comfort, a Super Mid-size aircraft offers a spacious cabin on this frequent business corridor.

What is the difference between DXB and DWC for private jets?

Dubai International (DXB) is highly congested with commercial airline traffic, leading to slot constraints and longer taxi times. Al Maktoum International (DWC) features the Mohammed bin Rashid Aerospace Hub, designed specifically for private aviation. DWC offers superior privacy, faster tarmac access, and dedicated VIP terminals with direct car-to-cabin boarding.

Are there last-minute private jet deals available from Dubai?

While we do not offer discounted "deals" in the traditional sense, we regularly secure empty leg repositioning flights. These flights occur when an aircraft needs to return to its home base or reposition for its next mission. When matching your schedule, empty legs can reduce standard charter rates by up to 75%.
