Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first enquiry

How far in advance do I need to book?
+
We recommend 48–72 hours for most charter requests, though our concierge team regularly handles same-day and urgent bookings. For peak seasons (summer Mediterranean, ski season, Grand Prix), 4–8 weeks advance notice ensures the best asset selection.
Is my information kept completely confidential?
+
Absolute discretion is foundational to our service. All client data is handled under strict NDA protocols. We never share, sell, or disclose client identities, travel plans, or personal information to any third party. Our partners are contractually bound to the same standards.
What are your pricing ranges?
+
Private jet charters start from $4,500 per flight hour for light jets. Yacht charters begin from $15,000 per week. Villa rentals start from $5,000 per night. All pricing is indicative — final quotes are personalised based on your exact requirements, dates, and destinations.
Which countries and regions do you serve?
+
We operate globally across 40+ countries, with particularly strong networks in the UAE, Europe (Mediterranean, France, Italy, Spain), Asia (Maldives, Thailand, Hong Kong), and the Americas. If you have a destination in mind, contact us — our reach is extensive.
How does the payment process work?
+
Upon receiving your tailored proposal, a deposit (typically 25–50%) secures your booking. The balance is due 7–14 days before departure. We accept bank transfers and can accommodate alternative arrangements for high-volume clients. Full transparency on all fees before commitment.
Can you arrange multiple services for one trip?
+
Absolutely — multi-modal itineraries are one of our specialities. We seamlessly coordinate private jet transfers, superyacht charters, and villa stays into one unified, frictionless experience managed through a single point of contact.
